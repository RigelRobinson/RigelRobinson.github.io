Adopt a Resolution approving the reimbursement of travel expenses at up to $6,000 from the discretionary Council Office Budgets of Mayor Arreguin and Councilmember Robinson for the purpose of visiting Berkeley&rsquo;s sister city, Gongju, Republic of Korea to officially commemorate the establishment of sister city relations.
